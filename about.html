Gentlemen’s Club commenced its journey in the year 2016 and experienced immense success and global recognition, right from its first edition. Gentlemen’s Club is a platform that is going to take the New Delhi men beyond just shopping. The event connects an eclectic mix of potential clients- the list of which includes HNI’s, Corporate Executives, Industrialists and other affluent individuals. Furthermore, we have developed and built a trusted platform between brands, buyers and retailers, solidifying bridges between the three different markets.

Gentlemen’s Club guarantees a distinctive show that will build new networks, relationships and experiences. Keeping in mind the different needs of diverse people, Gentlemen’s Club proposes a Beer Garden, Cigar Bar and also a Ladies Lounge at the venue will ensure that there would be enough avenues for each guest to relish the experience.
